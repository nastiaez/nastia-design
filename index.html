My superpowers

My Master's in Brand Management and experience as a media planner for LEGO taught me to design solutions that serve both users and business goals — not just pretty interfaces.

I've designed warehouse applications and B2B tools at Zalando — notoriously complex software requiring clarity for non-desk users. I don't shy away from hard problems.

I don't just design — I build. From Figma to HTML, Webflow sites, to vibe coding with Claude Code. I can take ideas from concept to working product.

Self-taught design systems via YouTube. Now exploring AI tools for ideation, wireframing, and code generation. I pick up new tools quickly and integrate them into my workflow.

I quantify design impact with metrics, conduct competitive analyses with pricing matrices, and write Google Sheets AI functions to extract insights. Design decisions backed by evidence.

I've built iconography-heavy design systems and organize complex information clearly. Structure and consistency are how I scale design work.
